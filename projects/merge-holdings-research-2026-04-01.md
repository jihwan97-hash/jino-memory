# Merge Holdings Research Update - April 1, 2026

## MiCA Regulation Context - EU Crypto Compliance 2025-2026

**Market-Wide Shifts:**
- MiCA (Markets in Crypto-Assets) regulation rolling out across EU
- Timeline varies by country:
  - Netherlands: July 2025 deadline
  - Italy: December 2025
  - Others: July 2026
- **70-75% of Europe's 3,167 VASPs facing consolidation pressure**

## Industry Trends (Not Merge-Specific)

**Compliance Challenges:**
- Expensive licensing requirements (EMI + VASP + CASP under MiCA)
- Many small players shutting down or relocating
- Mergers accelerating (smaller firms → bigger competitors)
- Collateralized lending now 90%+ of EU crypto lending (MiCA requirement)

**Proactive Countries:**
- Czech Republic, Malta, Bulgaria = clear transition laws
- Others = patchwork implementation (compliance gap)
