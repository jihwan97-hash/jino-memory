## Assessment

**Positive Signals:**
- Restructuring complete = organizational stability restored
- Clear strategic focus on Otherside
- Team expansion (100+ people) shows operational scale
- Metaverse roadmap with specific deliverables

**Concerns:**
- Still NO public metrics for VibeMaker adoption
- Legal battles continue (class-action + trademark disputes)
- NFT market headwinds persist
- Transparency remains poor compared to peers

**Investment Thesis:**
Yuga has survived 2022-2024 NFT winter and restructured successfully. Otherside is ambitious and has resources, but success depends on execution. The lack of metrics transparency makes it impossible to assess traction. Status remains Yellow until either (a) metrics are disclosed, or (b) negative signals emerge.
