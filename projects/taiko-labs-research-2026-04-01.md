# Taiko Labs Research Update - April 1, 2026

## Gwyneth Rollup - Next Generation Based Rollup

**Product Evolution:**
- Current: Taiko Alethia (Type-1 ZK-rollup mainnet)
- Future: **Taiko Gwyneth** (synchronous composable based rollup)
- Launch timeline: 2026 (devnet/testnet in 2025 completed)

## What is Gwyneth?

**Technical Architecture:**
- Based rollup = uses Ethereum for sequencing (not separate sequencer)
- Synchronous composability with L1 and other Gwyneth rollups
- Ethereum-native: publishing blocks, data availability, proof verification all on L1
- Goal: Solve fragmentation + interoperability in rollup-centric roadmap

**Key Differentiator:**
- Alethia = general-purpose Type-1 ZK-rollup
- Gwyneth = purist approach to uniting Ethereum L2s
- Synchronous = real-time cross-L2 composability (no bridges/delays)
