**Problem:** Rollup fragmentation and liquidity silos  
**Solution:** EIL for seamless cross-layer communication

**Key Features:**
- Trustless messaging system between rollups
- Fluid transactions without security compromises
- Reduced fragmentation across Ethereum L2s

**Taiko COO Quote (Joaquin Mendes):**  
EIL will provide "trustless messaging system, enabling fluid transactions and greater efficiency without compromising security."
