# Payhere Research - March 30, 2026

## Business Overview
Cloud-based mobile POS system for Korean SMEs
- Founded: 2018
- CEO: Park Jun-gi
- Product: Payhere Terminal (cloud POS app)

## Key Metrics
### Merchants
- **60,000+ merchants** reached (as of Oct 2024, Forbes Korea)
- Previous: 82,000+ reported in earlier marketing materials
- Target market: Small businesses to large franchises

### Funding History
- **Series A (Dec 2021):** .2M (100억원)
  - Investors: DST Global, Vision Plus Capital
- **Series B (Apr 2023):** 20억원
  - **Total raised:** ~120억원+ cumulative

## Product Features
- Zero monthly fees, transaction fees, penalty charges
- Cloud-based: tablets/smartphones (no physical hardware needed)
- Integrations: Naver reservations, real-time inventory, multi-location management
- **New (2025):** Tax refund service for foreign tourists

## Market Position
- #1 cloud POS in Korea
- Strong in cafes, restaurants, retail, salons
- Expanding to franchise chains (standardization focus)

## Recent Developments (2025-2026)
- Tourist-focused services (Seoul, Jeju, Busan, Gyeongju)
- Foreign tourist payment demand increasing
- Pop-up store short-term rental model gaining traction

**Status: GROWTH STAGE**
Active customer acquisition in tourism-heavy regions. Merchant count may have consolidated from 82K→60K (typical for SaaS churn/cleanup), but overall trajectory remains upward.

Sources: Crunchbase, KED Global, Forbes Korea, THE VC (2021-2024)
