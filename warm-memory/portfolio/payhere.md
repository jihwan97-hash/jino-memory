# Payhere (페이히어)
**Last Researched:** 2026-02-25

## Company Overview
- **Status:** 최우선 Priority - Revenue W131B
- **Sector:** Cloud Mobile POS, Payment Solutions
- **Market:** South Korea | CEO: Park Jun-ki (박준기)

## Recent Developments (2025-2026)

### Karrot Partnership (당근페이)
- Integrated with Karrot (당근), Korea's leading local community platform
- Payhere POS supports 'Karrot Pay' for neighborhood commerce marketing
- Strengthens local market penetration

### Pop-up Store Rental Service (May 2025)
- **New Product:** Short-term POS equipment rental for events/pop-up stores
- **Clients:** K-pop idol stores, Cafe Show, Illustrators Korea, beach festivals
- **Features:** Tax refund for tourists, WeChat Pay/Alipay, quick setup
- **Target:** Brands needing temporary payment infrastructure

### Platform Features
- Full customer flow: Reservation → Ordering → Payment → Customer Mgmt → Store Mgmt
- Industry-specific for F&B (prepaid/postpaid/kiosk/table order) and retail
- Naver Review integration (auto review requests)

## Strategic Outlook
- **Revenue:** ₩131B - significant scale
- **Growth:** Expanding from traditional POS to event/pop-up market
- **Differentiation:** Multi-currency support + tourist tax refund

## Competitive Position
- Strong local market (당근 partnership = community commerce access)
- First-mover in pop-up store rental
- Customer base: 3,000+ stores
