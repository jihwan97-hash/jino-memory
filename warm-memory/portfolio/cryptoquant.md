# CryptoQuant (팀블랙버드) Research

Last Researched: 2026-02-28

## Company Overview
- **Business:** Blockchain and crypto on-chain analytics platform
- **Korean Entity:** 팀블랙버드 (Team Blackbird)
- **Product:** On-chain and market analytics tools with actionable insights

## Recent Developments (2025-2026)

### Market Analysis & Thought Leadership
- **Bitcoin Cycle Projection (April 2024):** CryptoQuant published research projecting Bitcoin market cycle bottom between **June-December 2026**
- **Recent Coverage (Feb 2026):** Analysis still being cited and referenced in crypto media
- **Credibility:** Recognized as "leading blockchain analytics firm"

### Platform Capabilities
- **On-chain Analytics:** Comprehensive blockchain data analysis
- **Actionable Insights:** Professional analyst content and signals
- **Target Users:** Crypto traders, investors, institutions needing data-driven opportunities
- **Data Coverage:** Bitcoin, Ethereum, and major crypto assets

## Market Position
- **Competitive Landscape:** Competes with Glassnode, Nansen, Dune Analytics (also HVF portfolio!)
- **Differentiation:** Focus on actionable insights from top analysts, not just raw data
- **Brand Recognition:** Frequently cited in crypto media for market analysis

## Investment Considerations

**Strengths:**
- Established brand in crypto analytics
- Thought leadership position (market calls get media coverage)
- B2B and B2C revenue potential
- Crypto-native business aligned with industry growth

**Watch Points:**
- Crypto market volatility affects demand
- Competition from free tools (Dune) and premium (Glassnode)
- Need to track subscriber/revenue metrics
- Sustainability during bear markets

## Next Steps to Monitor
- Subscriber growth and retention
- Revenue model and pricing
- Product expansion (new chains, features)
- Strategic partnerships or data integrations
- Follow-on funding or profitability milestones
- Market share vs competitors (esp. Dune Analytics)
